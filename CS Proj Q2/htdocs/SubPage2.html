Climate change adversely affects agriculture and land use as unpredictable rainfalls and droughts will make it harder to make a profit as harvests decline. Severe drought makes it hard or impossible to grow crops especially in semi-arid or arid conditions.

Continuing declines in soil moisture can increase the need for irrigation in agriculture and lead to smaller yields and even desertification, with potentially dramatic impacts on food production
